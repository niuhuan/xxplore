If you run in Album Applet mode,
some features are limited by memory.
Some NSZ/XCZ files do not have enough memory to decompress.

When running Xxplore in application mode,
you can view larger images,
and you can also use a larger install cache.

Drive naming rules:
sdmc: means the SD card
ums*: means a USB storage device
Name(WebDAV): means a WebDAV drive
Name(SMB): means a Samba/SMB drive
Name(FTP): means an FTP drive
